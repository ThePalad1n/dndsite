Crab Race

Bonus to Str +2 Con +1 and Int -3.

Can speak one language of players choice but cannot read. Also can speak to other crustateans.

Cannot wear aromor or wield a weapon.

Natural armor 11 + str modifier + con modifier.

Weapon follows the weapon hit die chart.

Limited classes to Barbarian, Monk, or Fighter. Even with high Int the creature is still unable to comprehend more than fighting with its hands. Though nothing is impossible.

Every 4 levels after level 1 the crab grows one size and gains Str +1.

Lvl 1 Tiny Str

Lvl 5 Tiny => Small Str + 1

Lvl 9 Small => Medium Str + 2

Lvl 13 Medium => Large Str + 3

Lvl 17 Large => Huge Str + 4
