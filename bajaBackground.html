Wizard Dropout Background

This is for a fun twist on the traditional wizard play style/casting. You are a wizard that was trying to go to the wizarding academy. But to your suprise, even with great magical ability, you are unable to cast spells correctly so you drop out. You meet a tinkerer at an inn where he tell you he might be able to help. The neext day he takes you back to his workshop where he presents to you...


Varriation One: Spell Cannon


The spell cannon is a cannon that has dials for each spell slot and 1 normal cannon option. When switching to a spell level, the player can put the required components in the cannon as ammo and cast the spell. You must know the spell bbeing casted and must have a spell slot to cast it. Damage and range are also the same. The normal cannon is a 1d12 bldg at 30ft range. The catch of this class is that without the cannon the wizard cannot cast their spells, even with knowlegde of it.



Variaton Two: 10 Rings


each ring represents a level of spell from cantrip to level 9 and each hold a charge equal to their corresponding spell slots of that character. The chargges, like normal, recharge after a long rest. However, an unarmed attack deals 1d6 + str.
